Paikka is a location based mobile game. Paikka is
targeted at young adults, and it is designed to make
retailers, stores, restaurants and other local
businesses more visible, entertaining and to enhance
their customer satisfaction and loyalty.



Download App



For consumers

  • Paikka adds a game element to everyday tasks such as going to the supermarket
  • By completing quests set by real retailers you get both in-game and real life rewards
  • Paikka keeps track of your progress and helps you find new places to visit
  • It saves you money while you're having an awesome time!

For companies

  • Paikka brings in new customers and helps the regulars come to you more often
  • You can keep track of customer data and behavior to make marketing more accurate than ever
